What is FoodBank?
Foodbank is an online food delivery platform that allows users to order food from a wide range of restaurants and have it delivered to their doorstep.

How does Foodbank work?
Users can browse through the Foodbank website , choose a restaurant from the available options, select their desired dishes, and place an order. Foodbank then coordinates with the restaurant to prepare the order and assigns a delivery rider to pick up and deliver the food to the customer.

What restaurants are available on Foodbank?
Foodbank partners with a variety of restaurants,offering diverse cuisines to cater to different tastes and preferences.

Is Foodbank available in my area?
Not yet.

How can I pay for my Foodbank order?
Foodbank offers various payment options are cash on delivery and online payment, depending on your preferences.

How can I track my Foodbank order?
Foodbank provides order tracking features on its website , allowing users to monitor the status of their orders in real-time, from preparation to delivery.
